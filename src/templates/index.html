    In HELSINKI BILLIONS, you can buy and sell stocks from the ten companies most popular
    among Finnish households who have stockmarket investments. Create your portfolio
    and start buying and selling.

    Available are the years 2020-2021, a short, but interesting time.
    When you're done or at the end of 2021, the game is over and you can
    see how your portfolio did.

    Note there's a constraint that you can only sell "batches", i.e. sets of stocks
    from single buys. Sorry for the inconvenience that some dates are missing,
    this is due to using real Nasdaq data.

    When you start finding the exercise tedious, you have entered the zone.
    Successful investing involves some work. But you can actually lose thousands
    with the click of a button.

    Hope you enjoy! Thanks for playing!
